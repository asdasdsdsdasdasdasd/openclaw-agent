### Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS)
- **Provider**: Research Grants Council (RGC) / HKSAR Government, in partnership with CUHK
- **Type**: Research PhD / Government / International
- **Eligibility**: Open to all nationalities. Applicants must apply for full-time PhD admission at CUHK. Selection based on academic excellence, research ability, communication skills, leadership abilities, and cultural diversity. No citizenship/age restrictions stated.
- **Amount / Benefits**: Total ~HK$1.81M (~US$232,420) over normative period including:
  - Stipend: HK$344,400/yr (up to ~HK$1.38M total)
  - Conference & Research Travel Allowance: HK$14,400/yr (up to ~HK$87,600)
  - Tuition Fee Waiver: up to HK$198,000
  - Lodging Award: up to HK$100,000 (HK$40,000 first year + HK$20,000/yr subsequent)
  - 1st year Hostel Fee Waiver and Guaranteed On-campus Accommodation
- **Deadline**: RGC initial application: 1 Dec 2026 at 12:00 noon HKT. CUHK full application: 1 Dec 2026.
- **How to Apply**: Step 1: Submit initial application via [RGC HKPFS website](https://cerg1.ugc.edu.hk/hkpfs/InitApplication.jsp) for HKPFS Reference Number. Step 2: Submit full application to CUHK via [Online Application System](https://www.gradsch.cuhk.edu.hk/OnlineApp/login_email.aspx). Select up to 2 programmes. List CUHK as first choice to maximise chances.
- **Required Documents**: As per CUHK PhD admission requirements
- **Selection Criteria**: Academic excellence, research ability and potential, communication and interpersonal skills, leadership abilities, cultural diversity
- **Restrictions / Obligations**: If nominated by multiple universities, RGC only processes nomination from priority university. Not selected nominees automatically considered for CUHK Vice-Chancellor's PhD Scholarship.
- **Official Links**: https://www.gs.cuhk.edu.hk/admissions/scholarships-fees/hkpfs | https://cerg1.ugc.edu.hk/hkpfs/
- **Sources**: https://www.gs.cuhk.edu.hk/admissions/scholarships-fees/hkpfs (browser snapshot)
- **Notes**: For 2027-28 intake. Starting 2020-21 intake, all CUHK-nominated candidates not selected for HKPFS receive regular PhD place with VC PhD Scholarship.

### Hong Kong Future Talents Scholarship Scheme for Advanced Studies (FTSS)
- **Provider**: University Grants Committee (UGC) / HKSAR Government
- **Type**: Taught Postgraduate / Government / Local
- **Eligibility**: Local students (full-time and part-time) admitted to designated CUHK taught postgraduate (TPg) programmes for 2025/26 and 2026/27 intakes. Applications for 2026/27 invited in June 2026. Non-local students (student visa, IANG, dependant visa holders aged ≥18) not eligible. Students with prior government scholarships for same programme (double-benefit) excluded. Must hold admission offer, accept it, and pay admission deposit.
- **Amount / Benefits**: Awardees pay minimum tuition fee = prevailing UGC-funded full-time rate (HK$44,500 for 2025/26; HK$47,000 for 2026/27). Tuition difference subsidised up to **HK$100,000 cap** per student for entire programme. Scholarship used for tuition fee settlement only.
- **Deadline**: Varies by programme. Results announced by September 2026 for 2026/27 intake.
- **How to Apply**:
  1. Apply and gain admission to a designated TPg programme
  2. Contact respective Programme Office for FTSS application form
  3. Submit FTSS application (with HKID copy and supporting docs) to Programme Office by programme-specific deadline
- **Required Documents**: HKID copy, supporting documents as requested by Programme Office
- **Selection Criteria**: Academic achievements, professional qualifications, relevant working experience, proposal on contribution to HK/GBA priority areas. Merit-based. Universities assess holistically.
- **Restrictions / Obligations**:
  - Cannot hold other public tuition subsidies for same programme
  - Must sign written undertaking to contribute to relevant industries/sectors through full-time employment or entrepreneurial activities in HK or GBA upon graduation
  - Must submit mandatory return ≤12 months after last semester: employer/position/salary info AND one-page summary of contributions
  - Refund of full scholarship if mandatory return not submitted on time or info untrue
- **Official Links**: https://www.gs.cuhk.edu.hk/admissions/scholarships-fees/ftss | https://www.ugc.edu.hk/eng/ugc/activity/ftss.html | FAQ: https://www.ugc.edu.hk/eng/ugc/activity/ftss/faq.html
- **Sources**: CUHK GS FTSS page, UGC FTSS page, UGC FAQ page (browser snapshots)
- **Notes**: Up to 1,200 scholarships per year (1,000 for UGC-funded universities). Two cohorts so far: 2025/26 and 2026/27 intakes. See full eligible CUHK programme list in Government Schemes section below.

## Government Schemes (FTSS, HKSES, etc.)

### Hong Kong Future Talents Scholarship Scheme for Advanced Studies (FTSS)

**Background**: Government scheme introduced 2025/26 academic year by UGC to attract local students to pursue taught postgraduate programmes in priority areas.

**Priority Areas**: STEM, healthcare, sports, smart city/urban studies, ESG/sustainability, culture/arts/heritage, public administration/social sciences/social services, law/arbitration/mediation.

**Up to 1,200 scholarships/year** (1,000 for UGC-funded universities).

**Award Mechanism**: Student pays minimum tuition (HK$44,500 for 2025/26; HK$47,000 for 2026/27); FTSS subsidises the difference up to HK$100,000 cap per student for the whole programme.

**Post-award Obligations**: Signed undertaking to contribute to HK/GBA industries/sectors after graduation. Mandatory return ≤12 months after last semester with employment/entrepreneur info and one-page contribution summary. Full refund if not submitted or untrue.
